Una historia que te enseña español

Puerto Sol

You arrive in a small seaside town knowing no Spanish. Each scene teaches a few words first — tap ▶ to hear them — then the story uses only words you've learned. Tap any word you're unsure of and it gets extra practice; words you read past are assumed known. Between scenes, quick reviews bring words back just before you'd forget them.
